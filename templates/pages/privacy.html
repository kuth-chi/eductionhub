At Education Hub, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. We are committed to building trust through transparency and responsible data practices.

2. Hosting & Infrastructure

We use secure, industry-leading cloud infrastructure to host our services and store your data safely. Our providers comply with major international data protection standards, a global cloud infrastructure trusted for its compliance, and performance. We leverage cloud feature built-in protections to keep your data safe from unauthorized access, data loss, and cyber threats.

3. Authentication Services

To make access easier and more secure, we offer authentication via trusted third-party providers such as Google, Facebook, Telegram, and others. When you log in using one of these services, we only request essential information such as your name, email address, and profile image (if applicable). We do not access your passwords or any unnecessary personal data.

We use your data solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes allowing you to log in, personalize your experience, and access features like school search, scholarship listings, and saved preferences. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

6. Data Protection & Security

We apply strong industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data. These include encryption, secure authentication, regular audits, and ongoing monitoring. Our team is committed to protecting your privacy and responds swiftly to any security concerns.

You have full control over your data. At any time, you may:
