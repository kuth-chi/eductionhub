Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

1. Introduction

At Education Hub, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when using our platform and integrated third-party services such as Google APIs and Telegram authentication.

2. Third-Party Authentication & API Access

We use secure third-party authentication providers to make sign-in easier and safer:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: We request your consent to access basic profile data (name, email, profile photo) for authentication purposes. If you grant additional permissions (e.g., Google Drive, Calendar), we only use the data to provide the requested feature and follow Google's API Services User Data Policy and OAuth Limited Use Requirements.
  • Telegram Login: We authenticate using Telegram’s secure login system. We only receive your Telegram username, name, and profile picture (if available) for sign-in purposes. No private messages or contacts are accessed.

Important: We never access your passwords or share your data with third parties for advertising. You may revoke our access anytime via your Google Account or Telegram settings.

3. What We Collect

  • Basic account information (e.g., name, email, profile picture)
  • Google API data you authorize (e.g., Drive files, Calendar events) – only for features you request
  • Device, browser, and usage data for security and performance

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Provide features like file access via Google Drive or event sync with Google Calendar
  • Secure our platform and prevent misuse
  • Communicate important service updates

We comply with all applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA). We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising without consent.

5. Data Retention & User Controls

We store your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can revoke Google or Telegram access anytime through your account settings. Upon account deletion, all associated data is removed within 30 days unless legally required to retain it.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for students, parents, and educators. If you are under 13, please use the platform only with parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children without such consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via our website or email notifications. Please review this page regularly.

8. Contact Us
