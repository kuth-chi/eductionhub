Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

1. Introduction

At Education Hub, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. We are committed to building trust through transparency and responsible data practices.

2. Hosting & Infrastructure

We use secure, industry-leading cloud infrastructure to host our services and store your data safely. Our providers comply with major international data protection standards, a global cloud infrastructure trusted for its compliance, and performance. We leverage cloud feature built-in protections to keep your data safe from unauthorized access, data loss, and cyber threats.

3. Third-Party Authentication Services

To make access easier and more secure, we offer authentication via trusted third-party providers such as Google, Facebook, Telegram, and others. When you log in using one of these services:

  • Google OAuth: We access your basic profile info (name, email, profile picture) as permitted by Google's OAuth policies. We comply with Google's API Services User Data Policy.
  • Facebook Login: We request only essential permissions (public profile, email) in accordance with Facebook's Platform Terms and Data Policy.
  • Telegram Login: We authenticate via Telegram's secure login widget, accessing only your username, name, and photo if provided.

Important: We do not access your passwords, private messages, friends lists, or any unnecessary personal data. You can revoke our access at any time through your respective third-party account settings.

4. Data Processing Legal Basis

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you choose to log in via third-party services
  • Legitimate Interest: To provide, maintain, and improve our educational services
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our terms of service and provide requested features

5. What We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Basic identity data (e.g., name, email address, profile picture)
  • Account-related preferences and usage behavior (to improve your experience)
  • Browser and device data (collected anonymously for security and performance monitoring)

6. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes:

  • Enabling secure account creation and authentication
  • Personalizing your Education Hub experience
  • Providing access to educational resources and tools
  • Communicating important service updates
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We comply with all applicable data protection regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and platform-specific requirements.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal compliance.

8. Data Protection & Security

We apply strong industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data. These include encryption, secure authentication, regular audits, and ongoing monitoring. Our team is committed to protecting your privacy and responds swiftly to any security concerns.

9. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. At any time, you may:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw your consent to data collection (where applicable)
